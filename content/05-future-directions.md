---
title: Future Directions
order: 5
---

CDIF is an intentionally evolving standard. We are constantly looking to cover gap areas and perfect the foundation to support automated, cross-domain FAIR exchange. 

The next major release is expected in **mid-2026**. Upcoming releases will focus on:

- Supporting the detailed description of a broader range of data formats, including **NetCDF, HDF5, Parquet, JSON, and XML**.
- Recommendations for describing provenance using the **PROV Ontology**.
- Packaging of resources explicitly with **RO Crates** and other associated standards.
- Enhanced capabilities around the description of **Data Context**.
- Expanding use cases for describing data for **AI systems**, such as formatting training data.
