The development of CDIF is the result of years of international collaboration aimed at solving the "last mile" problem of data interoperability. It builds upon established domain-neutral standards to create a practical framework for cross-domain data exchange.

*   **History of CDIF**: CDIF emerged from the WorldFAIR project and subsequent CODATA initiatives.
*   **A Solid Foundation**: It integrates lessons learned from early implementations and pilot projects across various scientific domains.
