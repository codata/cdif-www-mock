CDIF is a community-driven initiative that operates under a robust governance framework to ensure its long-term sustainability and relevance.

*   **Directed by CODATA**: The framework is strategically directed by [CODATA](https://codata.org), the Committee on Data of the International Science Council.
*   **Governed by AG and WG**: Technical and strategic decisions are made by the Advisory Group (AG) and various Working Groups (WG).
*   **Community Intellectual Property**: All original work produced within CDIF is held by CODATA on behalf of the global community.
*   **Open Licensing**: CDIF recommendations and documentation are licensed under **CC-BY 4.0**, encouraging wide adoption and reuse.
