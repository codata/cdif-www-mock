First published in May 2024 as an output from the WorldFAIR Project, CDIF is now developed and maintained by a group of invited experts, coordinated by CODATA. 

It is designed to be implementable with existing tools, standards, and technologies. Rather than outlining a theoretical best possible solution, CDIF advocates for standards and technology *already in common use* so that FAIR exchange can become as easy and widespread as possible.

It is increasingly being implemented as part of projects in Europe, Africa, and globally, in a number of disparate domains. Increasingly, CDIF tools are being developed and made available. Examples of active adoptions and implementations include:

- **CDIF4XAS**
- **ClimateAdapt4EOSC**
- **Dataverse**
- **Nectar**
- **Deeptime Digital Earth**
- **PSDI**
- **High Value Data Network**
- **Data Artifex**
